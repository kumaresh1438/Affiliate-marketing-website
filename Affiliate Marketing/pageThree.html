At this time and day, it is that simple for anyone to create their own affiliate
marketing website. So, to set you apart from the competition, here are 15 proven
tips and tricks for Affiliate Marketing Websites

1. Build Trust with your Visitors

The number one tip in affiliate marketing is to establish a relationship with your
visitors. They need to have enough trust to purchase products from your affiliate
marketing website.

You need to establish that you have sound knowledge of the product you are
promoting, enough even to recommend it to others. So, by regularly writing on a
particular niche, you showcase your expertise and also build a sense of trust with
your visitors.

Also, another way of building trust with your visitors is by disclosing the fact that
you are using an affiliate link. By doing so, they will just know that you are
promoting a particular product, which won't affect the purchase if they are
genuinely interested in the product.

2. Know the Protects

This goes without saying, but selling a product you are familar with solely for
the affiliate commision doesn't hold good in the long run. You should know about
the protect throughly before recommending it to your visitors

In other words, you need to have a complete understanding of the product before
adding an affiliate link to your website. In fact, you should only be promoting the
product you have already tried for yourself. This not just helps with the trust factor
but also allows you to answer any queries by your visitors.

In case you don't do so, the unfamiliarity with the product will start to show up in the
content and thus breaks the trust of your visitors.

3. Create Great Content

This goes without saying, but selling a product you are familar with solely for
the affiliate commision doesn't hold good in the long run. You should know about
the protect throughly before recommending it to your visitors

Though affiliate marketing in its core revolves around the product/service, what
drives your visitors to make the purchase is the engaging content you create.
Content is not necessarily limited to the promotional text you write. You can even
include images, videos related to the product.

Or, you can even take it up a notch by providing a free Ebook that's around the same
product niche. Such high-quality content can help generate more traffic to your
affiliate marketing website. So, it is important to first focus on the content and then
the affiliate part that comes with it.

4. Provids Ratings & Score

Everyone reviews the product, and thus the promotional content gets pretty
repetitive for the visitors over time. So, how can you stand out from the
competition?

You can try to include Ratings, be it in the form of Star ratings or even a score out
of 100, along with the usual product review. Providing such ratings will make your
content not just informational but helpful for the visitors to make a sound decision.

Apart from making your content unique and helpful, ratings prove your expertise
and thus increases the credibility of your affiliate marketing website.

5. Get Creative with the CTAS

If there is a single most crucial part of your affiliate marketing website, it has to be
the Call To Action (CTA), telling the visitors to visit the affiliate link. As we are
already using a separate button for the purpose, try to get creative with the words
and colors.

Adding or replacing a single word in the CTA may impact a visitor's decision to click
on the affiliate link. So, you need to think twice before settling on the magic word
that settles the deal.

Also, colors play a huge role in decision-making. So, you need to pick a color that's
appealing and nicely contrasts with your website background.

6. Make use of Social Media

Though most traffic for affiliate marketing websites comes organically, you should
not underestimate the potential of social media. Social media can play a massive
role in redirecting a chunk of traffic to your affiliate marketing website.

You can make use of the same affiliate content and optimize it for different social
media platforms. This will help drive more visitors from multiple social media
streams to your website.

This added traffic from social media can definitely have an impact on the overall
revenue of the affiliate marketing website.

7. Use Emails to your Advantage

Emails have a great Return Of Investment (ROI) and are a perfect way to engage
your subscribers and generate traffic to your affiliate marketing website. With more
traffic, more and more people will get to read your content, and thus it increases the
potential sale that happens through the affiliate link.

You shouldn't just stop at sending promotional content about the product but also
send some content that adds value to them and encourages them to check out the
product.

So, instead of including the affiliate link directly on the Email, use it as a form of
diverting your subscribers towards the affiliate marketing website.

8. Improve the User Experience of your Website

Though User experience is something every website owner should focus on, it's
even more important when it comes to affiliate marketing websites. Because better the
user experience, more chances of sales through affiliate links.

A lot of factors come into play when we talk about user experience. However, the
top ones being, having a mobile-friendly website that's responsive on all devices,
having readable content without any distractions, and even a fast website that
doesn't keep the visitors waiting.

9. Have a Clean User Interface

Talking of readable content, you also need to concentrate on the User Interface,
which refers to how the content appears on your website. This constitutes
everything that appears on a website, including the placement of text, images, and
even elements like buttons.

A clean user interface removes any kind of distractions and lets your visitors
concentrate on the content and, most importantly, the CTA.

10. Focus on SEO

It is quite easy for beginners to start a blog, write content, and even attach affiliate
links to the content. But they always end up with the question, How can I bring
traffic to affiliate links?

Well, while there are several ways to drive traffic, the first thing you need to
concentrate on is SEO. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is optimizing your
website to rank higher on the search results, thus driving more traffic organically.

So, right after you link the affiliate on the website, you need to ensure that your
website is search engine optimized. Again there are several ways to optimize your
website, be it On-Page SEO (optimizations directly on your website) or Off-Page SEO
(indirect optimizations)

For starters, you can start by having a better user interface and improved user
experience on your website. This will help your website rank higher in the search
results and thus will improve the conversions.

11. A/B Test your Affiliate Marketing Content

Choosing the right keywords and writing content around that isn't enough for
affiliate marketing. You need to be sure of whether a particular content will work or
not with your visitors. So, the guessing game isn't enough when you are working on
affiliate marketing content.

You need to know what exactly works with your website visitors. And A/B testing is
the way to do that. A/B testing is when you test two versions of the content, namely
A & B, to see which one performs better with your visitors. This content can be
anything from a headline to an eye-catching CTA.

At the end of the test, you can go ahead with the one version that performed well
over the other. This removes the guessing game and is sure to work with the
visitors that will result in a potential sale.

12. Offer Discounts & Free Services

Discounts and free services are effective techniques used in the marketing world,
especially applicable for affiliate marketing. You can offer some sort of deals and
discounts on the products or provide free services that go well with the affiliate
products you sell on the website.

Having something unique to offer to your visitors will make your website stand out
from the other affiliate marketing websites that only sell the product.

13. Experiment with Different Affiliate Programs

More often than you imagine, we get the question, What is the best affiliate
marketing program for beginners?

Well, there isn't a single affiliate program that works for everybody. Sometimes, an
affiliate program just won't produce the intended sales for you. At times like that,
when you can't exactly figure out why it doesn't work, you need to try and start
experimenting with the other affiliate programs available.

To make things easier, we have compiled a list of the best affiliate programs,
including popular ones like Amazon, Fiverr, etc., that are available in the market.
Signing up with the different affiliate programs will definitely open up more
opportunities for affiliate commission.

14. Look at your Competition

Yes, you definitely need to have a look at the competition that's already performing
well in your niche and analyze what makes their content work. You need to
thoroughly understand how your competitors write the content, place the CTA, and
much more.

And of course, instead of copying the strategy blatantly, you need to think about how
you can improve and make it better for the visitors. You should always focus on
taking it up a notch on your website.

15. Patience , Patience , and more Patience

Just like Rome, your affiliate marketing business can't be built in a day. Even after
putting in all the effort, you may end up with no sales initially. That is when you need
to understand that this is a long-term process, and you need to be patient

Even after putting in all the work, testing , and updating the content and CTAs, you
still need to wait. You need to understand that the right startegies will bring in more
sales, even though it may take a while initially to reach there
